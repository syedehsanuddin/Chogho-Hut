about us

Welcome to Chogho Sar Huts and Camping Point, your perfect escape nestled in the serene beauty of Upper Kachura. Located on the picturesque Soq Valley Road, just 2km from the renowned Shangrila Resort and near the main bazar of Kachura, our resort offers a tranquil retreat with breathtaking views and modern comforts.

At Chogho Sar Huts and Camping Point, we offer two types of accommodations to cater to your needs:

1. Deluxe Rooms: Enjoy the spacious comfort of our Deluxe Rooms, featuring a master bed in a 14×16 ft room with an attached 7×14 ft washroom. These rooms provide a luxurious stay with stunning views and ample space to relax.

2. Budget Rooms: Our Budget Rooms are designed for comfort and convenience, each with a master bed in a 12×14 ft room and a 4.5×12 ft washroom. These rooms are perfect for travelers seeking a cozy and affordable stay without compromising on quality.

Our resort is surrounded by the natural beauty of Kachura, offering magnificent views of the Kachura River, towering mountains, and lush gardens. Whether you are here to unwind, explore, or simply enjoy nature, Chogho Sar Huts and Camping Point provides the perfect backdrop for a memorable experience.

frequently asked questions

What amenities are available at Chogho Sar Huts and Camping Point?

We offer a variety of amenities including complimentary WiFi, parking, a beautifully maintained garden, and an on-site restaurant serving local and international cuisines.

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is from 10:00 AM onwards, and check-out is max by 11:00 AM. If you need a late check-out, please inform the reception in advance, and we will do our best to accommodate your request.

Are pets allowed at the resort?

Yes! Pets are allowed at Chogho Sar Huts and Camping Point

Do the rooms have views?

Yes, our rooms offer beautiful views of the Kachura River, mountains, and our lush garden, providing a scenic and tranquil environment for your stay.

How far is the resort from Shangrila Resort and the main bazar in Kachura?

Chogho Sar Huts and Camping Point is conveniently located just 2km from Shangrila Resort and near the main bazar in Kachura, making it easy for guests to explore the local area and its attractions.
